FAQ

"How much do lessons cost?"

Typically $30.00 per 45 minutes. However, if you get six lessons, price is $150.00 for all six. (This is the typical "package")

"Do you offer refunds?"

Each case is considered. If there are valid reasons (e.g. life circumstances, physical problems that prohibit you from learning, etc) then a rebate will be given. However, rebates are only given for packages (6 lessons) and then only for lessons you have not attended. Refunds will not be given for completed lessons.

"Am I too old to learn how to play?"

You are never too old to learn. However, there might be physical issues that may make it difficult. In those cases, there may be some work-arounds (alternate ways to play chords) that you will be shown. If you determine that you cannot complete you lesson package, you can ask for a refund for those classes you have not completed.

"What will I learn?"

The six lesson package will teach you the very basics of the guitar. Holding, tuning, strumming. You will learn 3 chords and one song (possibly more based upon how fast you're learning). But in the process you will have built a foundation to go forward on your own.You will also be given resources to continue improving at the end.
